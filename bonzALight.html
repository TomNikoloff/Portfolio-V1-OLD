Bonz-A-Light wanted to provide a solution that brought nature indoors,
without the usual upkeep of indoor plantlife, while offering a stylish and usable product.

Closed Cycle

The Bonz-A-Light is hermetically sealed. The water within cannot escape into its surroundings. It evaporates and condenses in a closed cycle that keeps the plantlife inside sufficiently wet.

Easy Removal

If you fancy a change, plants can be removed within seconds and replaced with a entirely new plant from within our range.

Sunless

The Bonz-A-Light is a entirely self-supporting ecosystem using purpose built LEDs to imitate the sunlight a plant would usually require.

How Its Made

Handcrafted in germany, Bonz-A-Light are a tribute to traditional glassmaking and a combination of nature, technology and design. They embody Bonz-A-Lights philosophy of rejuvenating traditional crafts and trades by fusing them with modern technologies and cooperating with regional manufacturing companies to produce high-end, timeless pieces of furniture.
